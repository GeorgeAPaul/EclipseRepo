~~~~~Welcome to Unnamed Pirate Game 3!!!!~~~~~
In this game you play as the Unnamed Pirate, your objective is to try to defeat all the enemies on the map.
The map is generated randomly each time you play, feel free to play this game 1 million times to experience all the combinations!

You probably aren't strong enough to win yet...maybe you should try to find some weapons...maybe a map would help...

If you ever get stuck you can consult the guide by typing 'guide'
